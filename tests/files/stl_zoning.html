Zoning Section

What We Do

The Zoning Section reviews all occupancy and building permits for compliance with the Zoning Code which is located in Title 26 of the 1994 City of St. Louis Revised Code. 

Permits are either approved as a Use By Right, referred to a Conditional Use hearing or Denied; applicants can appeal a Denial to the Board of Adjustment. 

We also review & process the following:

  • Zoning Verification Letters
  • Home Occupancy Waivers
  • Incidental Business Waivers
  • Sign Permits
  • Rezonings
  • Land Subdivisions/Consolidations
  • Plat & Petition signatures
  • Zoning Overlay Districts
    • Form-Based Districts - Central West End & Forest Park Southeast
    • Special Use Districts
    • Planned Unit Development Districts
    • Community Unit Plans

For additional information regarding any of the above items, please see the Zoning FAQ or contact Zoning Staff at 314-622-3666.
